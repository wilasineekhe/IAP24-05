Warm Undertone

Dark green or olive veins and a yellowish skin tone are common characteristics found in Asian skin tones.

Cool Undertone

Blue or purple veins and a light skin tone with a pinkish, not yellow, undertone.

Neutral Undertone

Green, dark blue, and mixed veins, along with a medium skin tone, are typically associated with neutral undertones neither pink nor yellow
