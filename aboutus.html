Objectivs

This website is designed to help you discover the best cosmetics that match your unique skin tone. You can test the skin tone you believe fits you by visiting the linked section under Tone. If you're interested in exploring research articles about cosmetics and skin tones, head over to the Research section. Once you've determined your skin tone, simply click on the Cosmetic section to browse through the products we've handpicked just for you. This website was developed as a project for the Interaction Programming course during our second year in the Faculty of Engineering at Khon Kaen University. We hope this website brings you joy and helps you find the perfect cosmetics that complement your skin tone!
